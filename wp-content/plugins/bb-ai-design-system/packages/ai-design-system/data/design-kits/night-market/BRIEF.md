# Night Market — Concept Brief

## Identity

- **Name:** Night Market
- **Slug:** night-market
- **UUID:** 44bdc824-1d8f-40e9-8bfd-13c38cdc55f6
- **Description:** A Taipei night market after the rain — neon signage, handwritten menu boards, steam off hawker carts.

## Concept

A food tour operator / supper club brand set in the sensory overload of East Asian night markets — Shilin, Raohe, Jalan Alor. Wet pavement reflecting signs, handwritten price boards, plastic stools, the clatter of woks. The site sells guided tours and a recipe zine. The feeling: kinetic, crowded, generous — not polished or tourist-brochure.

## Palette direction

- Ground: wet asphalt (near-black), cold steel
- Ink: bone white, rice paper
- Accent: electric pink (neon sign glow), mango yellow
- Secondary: jade green, chili red

## Typography direction

- **Heading feel:** heavy condensed display, almost signage-like — high impact, slight imperfection
- **Body feel:** crisp humanist sans with good weight range for hierarchy
- **Preferred Google Fonts:** Archivo Narrow, Oswald, or Anton for display / Inter or Work Sans for body
- **Supplementary:** a mono for prices, times, stall numbers (JetBrains Mono or Space Mono)

## Decorative vocabulary

- Neon-glow text effect on select headings (pink/yellow outer glow on dark fields)
- Ticket-stub style cards with dashed tear edges for tour items
- Handwritten-feel accents via bold italic or marker-style weight for prices and specials
- Stall numbers (No. 04, No. 17) as tab-style labels
- High-contrast photography with wet-street reflection feel
- Diagonal stripes (caution-tape rhythm) as section dividers

## Voice and tone

Kinetic, generous, specific. "Queue for the oyster omelette. Skip the bubble tea you've already had. Trust the uncle with the cleaver — he's been here longer than you've been alive."

## Scope for this kit

- **Landing page:** Tour operator landing — hero with tonight's tour, featured stalls, testimonials, booking CTA
- **Inner page:** `tours.html` — list of available tours by market/city with prices and times
- **Style guide:** always built
- **Header + footer:** always built
- **Art direction doc:** always built (written after the landing page)

## Anti-patterns

- No "Asian-fusion" cliché — no bamboo, no dragons, no stock red lanterns.
- No clean minimal whitespace — this kit is dense, layered, noisy.
- No centered hero text on pastel backgrounds.
- No pale photography — images must feel wet, saturated, nocturnal.
