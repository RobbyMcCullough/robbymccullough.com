# Concept Brief — Ridgeline Estate

## Identity

- **Name:** Ridgeline Estate
- **Slug:** ridgeline-estate
- **UUID:** 261c6f7e-e1b3-4c47-ac49-9262ef7687b1
- **Description:** A Napa Valley winery — estate-grown Cabernet, hillside vineyards at golden hour, unhurried tastings.

## Concept

A three-generation family winery on the eastern ridge of Napa Valley, est. 1978. The posture is unhurried and quietly confident — old vines, French oak, wine that's ready when it's ready. "Come as a guest, leave as family." This kit is promoted directly from the Ridgeline Estate showcase page.

## Palette direction

Deep navy as the ground, gold as the accent — the gold shows up in eyebrows, rules, buttons, and hairlines. Cream and pale gold appear sparingly as warm highlights. Text sits on dark surfaces most of the page.

## Typography direction

Cormorant Garamond for display (italicized pull words — "Vineyard", "Honest Wine"). DM Sans for body, labels, and navigation. Labels run all-caps with wide tracking.

## Decorative vocabulary

- Hairline gold rule preceding eyebrow labels
- Ghost year marks (oversized "1978" bleeding behind hero content)
- Roman numerals anchoring story sections (I, II, III)
- Est. year badges on imagery
- Reveal animation on scroll (opacity + rise)

## Scope for this kit

- **Landing page:** `index.html` — hero, story, tasting room, wine collection, vineyard quote, events, visit. Sourced directly from the Ridgeline showcase page.
- **Inner page:** `our-story.html` — narrative About page expanding the homepage's Our Story section (founders, the land, three generations, how we make wine).
- **Style guide:** `style-guide.html` — identity card + showcase sections (palette, typography, buttons, decorative vocabulary, cards, spacing, image treatment).
- **Header + footer:** `globals/header.html` and `globals/footer.html`, identical to the original inline versions.
- **Art direction:** `design-system/art-direction.md` — observational document covering palette rationale, typography, decorative vocabulary, anti-patterns, voice, and business context.
