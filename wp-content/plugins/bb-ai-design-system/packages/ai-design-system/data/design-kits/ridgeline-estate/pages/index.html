Three Generations
of Honest Wine

Harold and June Callahan planted the first vines on this hillside in the fall of 1978 — a 40-acre estate carved from Napa Valley’s eastern ridge, where the afternoon fog rolls in slow and the days end long.

Today their grandchildren tend the same rows. We make Cabernet the way we always have: picked by hand, aged in French oak, bottled when it’s ready. The valley changes around us. The wine stays honest.
