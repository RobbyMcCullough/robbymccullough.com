I. Palette

A Single Navy Ground,
Gold as the Only Accent

The entire palette operates in the navy register. Gold does the signal work — hairlines, eyebrows, button fills, and the italic pull-word inside headings. White with alpha handles every tier of quiet-to-loud text.

II. Typography

Cormorant Sets the Voice,
DM Sans Carries the Rest

Display, stat numbers, and pull quotes live in Cormorant Garamond at light weights. Body, eyebrows, buttons, and metadata live in DM Sans. Italic inside headings is the only emphasis device.

VI. Spacing Scale

A Six-Step Rhythm,
Section Space Holds Long

Spacing tokens run from a 0.5rem breath to a 7rem section rest. Most layouts lean on lg and xl for inside-panel rhythm; section anchors the vertical space between major regions.

VII. Image Treatment

Three Ways Photography
Meets the Navy Ground

Every image sits under a navy gradient or veil. The bottom-fade carries hero type. The horizontal dissolve marries a photo to an adjacent text panel. The whole-image dim pushes the photograph into mood.
