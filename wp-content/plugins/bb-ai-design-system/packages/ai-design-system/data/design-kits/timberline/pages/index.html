Après Ski Bar

Craft cocktails, local beers, and a whiskey list that makes après the best part of the day. Open from 3 PM until late.

Outdoor Hot Tub

Soaking at 104° while snow falls around you is one of life's great experiences. Open until midnight, seven days a week.

Gear Storage Room

Heated individual lockers, boot dryers, and a wax station. Your gear is ready when you are, and dry when you get back.

Mountain Breakfast

A proper sit-down breakfast served 6:30–9:30 AM. Because you shouldn't charge a mountain on an empty stomach.

Game Room

Pool table, shuffleboard, pinball, and good music. For when the night is young and the mountain is still in your legs.

Lift tickets, rental gear coordination, and local intel on what's running, what's worth the hike, and where to eat after.
